IxiBlobs Page

Here's a map with blobs showing the distribution of some members of the Ixion Mailing List, a UK-centric forum for the discussion of motorcycles. You can click on some of the blobs to go to that Ixi's homepage or email address or whatever. Red IxiBlobs are the exclusive preserve of the proud members of the list-within-a-list, Ixion Self Help In Trouble (InSHIT)

We also have a few members around the world,  in Europe,  America and Canada and  Australia.

The blobs represent the locations of only about a quarter of Ixies, but they're the most prolific quarter, so it represents at least 90% of messages. For extra fun, the Green circles show the locations of some jolly nice race tracks. Click on 'em and go there, courtesy of BMRC.

If you're an Ixi and you'd like a blue or red IxiBlob of your own, mail me with your town / postcode , whether you're a member of the InSHIT  list, and what (if any) URL you'd like associated with your blob.
