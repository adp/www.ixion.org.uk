How do I stop the sun getting in my eyes?

Depends on the height of the sun. One approach is to put insulting [sic]
tape along the top edge of your visor (I used two widths on my
Shoei, haven't got round to doing it on the HJC). Acts as a sun
visor like what cars have. Tilting the head down brings the shade
down, raising it allows the sun to stream into your eyes and blind
you.
