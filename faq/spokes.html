Any tips for truing spokes?

>  Has anyone got any good tips for trueing (spoked, obviously :) wheels?
Just progressively tighten some spokes (should be obvious which ones) until the wheel comes back to true, then check all spoke tensions afterwards. Test trueness by spinning the wheel on the axle.

Turned out to be much easier than expected on my old DT175.

If the spokes have never been adjusted, some are probably seized, and will need replacing. You might get away with soaking in WD40 first.
