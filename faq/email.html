What kind of stuff should I e-mail?

Stuff to do with motorcycling, trip reports, technical assistance, organising meets, whatever, the key word is Motorcycling. It is not a forum for recycling antique homilies from alt.worst.of.internet, and it is not a chatline. Keep the frivolous stuff out. Although the conversation should be thought of as a group of bikers talking in a pub. The topics can go off at tangents sometimes.
