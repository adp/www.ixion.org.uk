Start of Season

At the start of the riding season for non-RTBs - some of us ride all year round! - we have our Start of Season bash. Since time immoral it's been held at the Baskerville Hall Hotel near Hay on Wye.
It's a great place to meet being, as it is, adjacent to excellent roads for riding in Wales, and close enough to Hay on Wye and its collection of excellent bookshops, various eateries and enough pubs to keep us satisfied. It's adjacent enough that it's a comfortable walk from and to the hotel.
