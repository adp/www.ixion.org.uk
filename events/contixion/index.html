Contixion

A motly crew of ixies descend on Normandy for an extended long weekend.  We stay that the Normandy Beach which run by a very friendly English couple, and is very bike friendly.

Sometimes in May, sometimes in June or July.  While it's not always pissheads and food bravado, it is certainly good fun.

This year we have another option of the 12th - 14th October.
