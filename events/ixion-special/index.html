Ixion Special

The annual opportunity for Ixies, their friends and relations to spend the weekend amidst glorious scenery at the Talyllyn Railway. The local roads are motorcycle heaven, there's the railway itself, the first in the world to be saved and operated by volunteers, and there's the beach and all the other trappings of the seaside. The highlight of the affair is a ride up the valley on our chartered train to Abergynolwyn where we burn things on the BBQs and have a couple of barrels of something tasty.

A grand weekend away and one of the more family-friendly Ixion events.

Ixion Special XIII will take place this year on Saturday the 1st of July 2017.
