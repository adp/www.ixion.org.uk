Absent Friends

Some years ago an Ixie called Paul suggested a late September weekend's camping at a rather splendid North Yorkshire pub he knew.

Lots of Ixies went and a Jolly Good Time was had - such a good time, in fact, that a repeat performance was scheduled for the following year.

But before the year was out he had a crash on his motorcycle and died.

He was the first but hasn't been the last :-((

The pub we used to meet in isn't suitable anymore and only a few of us camp these days, but year on year we still meet up in late September, in North Yorkshire - these days at Beck Hall in Malham. It's not a melancholy affair - none of the friends we've lost would appreciate that! We talk le bolleaux, we drink ale, scoff excellent nosh and generally have a great weekend, but we also raise a glass now and then to our Absent Friends.
